About Simple Meditation Timer

Simple Meditation Timer is a minimalist meditation app designed to help you practice mindfulness without distractions. Set your timer, meditate, and track your progress - it's that simple.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set a custom timer duration?
Tap the "Custom" button on the main screen, then use the picker wheels to select your desired minutes and seconds.
Where is my meditation history stored?
All your meditation sessions are stored locally on your device. We don't collect or store any personal data on external servers.
Will the timer continue if I close the app?
The app will send you a notification when your meditation session completes, even if the app is in the background.
How do I view my meditation history?
Tap the "History" button in the top-right corner to see all your completed meditation sessions and statistics.
Can I delete my meditation history?
Your meditation history is stored locally on your device. You can clear it by deleting and reinstalling the app.
Does the app work on iPad?
Yes! The app is optimized for both iPhone and iPad with responsive layouts for the best experience on any device.

Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. Simple Meditation Timer:

  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not track your usage or behavior
  • Stores all data locally on your device
  • Does not share data with third parties
  • Does not require an account or login

Troubleshooting

The timer isn't working properly
Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the problem persists, restart your device.
I'm not receiving notifications
Check that notifications are enabled for Simple Meditation Timer in your device's Settings > Notifications.
The app crashes or freezes
Make sure you have the latest version of the app. If problems continue, try restarting your device.
