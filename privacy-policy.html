Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 7, 2025

Effective Date: January 7, 2025

üîí Your Privacy is Our Priority

Simple Meditation Timer respects your privacy and does not collect, store, or share any personal information. This app is designed to work entirely on your device without any data transmission to external servers.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Simple Meditation Timer is built with privacy by design. We do not collect:

  • Personal Information: No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact details
  • Usage Analytics: No tracking of how you use the app
  • Device Information: No device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data
  • Meditation Data: Your meditation sessions are not transmitted anywhere
  • Behavioral Data: No tracking of app interactions or user behavior
  • Advertising Data: No data for advertising purposes (we don't show ads)

2. Local Data Storage

All your meditation data is stored locally on your device:

  • Meditation History: Session durations and timestamps are saved only on your device
  • App Preferences: Your settings and preferences remain on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not sync your data to any cloud services
  • No Backups: We do not create backups of your data on external servers

Note: If you delete the app, all your meditation history will be permanently removed from your device.

3. Permissions

Simple Meditation Timer only requests the following permission:

  • Notifications: To alert you when your meditation session is complete

You can disable notifications at any time through your device's Settings without affecting the app's core functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

Simple Meditation Timer does not integrate with any third-party services:

  • No Analytics Services: We don't use Google Analytics, Firebase, or similar services
  • No Advertising Networks: No ads means no ad tracking
  • No Social Media Integration: No sharing to social platforms
  • No Cloud Services: No data sent to AWS, Google Cloud, or other cloud providers

5. Children's Privacy

Simple Meditation Timer is safe for users of all ages:

  • We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13
  • The app does not require any personal information to function
  • No account creation or registration is required
  • The app is designed to be used safely by children with parental guidance

6. Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, there's no risk of data breaches:

  • No Data Transmission: Your information never leaves your device
  • Local Encryption: iOS automatically encrypts data stored on your device
  • No Servers: We don't maintain any servers that could be compromised
  • No Accounts: No usernames or passwords to be stolen

7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, you automatically have:

  • Complete Control: All your data stays on your device
  • Right to Delete: Simply delete the app to remove all data
  • No Data Requests: There's no data for us to provide or delete
  • Transparency: This policy explains exactly what we do (and don't do)

8. International Users

This privacy policy applies to all users worldwide:

  • GDPR Compliance: We comply with EU privacy regulations by not collecting data
  • CCPA Compliance: California residents have no personal data to be concerned about
  • Global Standards: We meet the highest privacy standards by design

9. App Store and iOS

Apple may collect some information through the App Store:

  • App downloads and updates are handled by Apple's App Store
  • Apple's privacy policy governs any data they collect
  • We do not have access to any App Store analytics or user data
  • iOS crash reports (if enabled) are sent to Apple, not to us

10. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices:

  • We will update this policy and the "Last Updated" date
  • We will notify users through an app update if significant changes occur
  • We remain committed to not collecting personal data
  • Any changes will maintain or improve your privacy protection

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy:

Email: privacy@simplemeditation.app

Support: support@simplemeditation.app

We typically respond within 24-48 hours.
